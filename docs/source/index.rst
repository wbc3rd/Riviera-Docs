Welcome to Riviera!
===================================

Riviera, Colorado State University’s high-performance computing (HPC) system, is a powerful resource designed to accelerate research and innovation across disciplines. Managed by the Data Science Research Institute, Riviera is open to all CSU researchers and students, providing advanced computational capabilities to tackle complex scientific challenges. Whether working on AI-driven projects or bioinformatics research, users can harness Riviera’s robust infrastructure to push the boundaries of discovery.
Equipped with 16 A100 GPUs for AI computations and high-memory CPU nodes optimized for bioinformatics, Riviera empowers researchers to process massive datasets, train sophisticated machine learning models, and perform intensive simulations with efficiency. By fostering collaboration and computational accessibility, Riviera strengthens CSU’s commitment to advancing research across diverse fields, enabling breakthroughs in data science, biology, engineering, and beyond.


Check out the :doc:`usage` section for further information, including
how to :ref:`installation` the project.
